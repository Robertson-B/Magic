How the commander pod creator works

Use the home page to enter a tournament name, choose the player count, and paste player names one per line. The app builds four-player pods across 4 rounds and makes the finals cut obvious from the standings.

Pod creation

The player count controls the number of seats. Every pod is four players wide, and any empty seats are filled with BYEs automatically.

Scoring

After generating pods, enter a score for each player and calculate advancement. The highest score in each pod moves on to the next round preview.

Next useful additions

  • Persist saved pods and scores in SQLite.
  • Add tie-breaking rules for tied scores.
  • Support multiple scoring rounds automatically.
